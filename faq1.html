PREFABRICATED

Our Prefabricated construction method is a process of building a structure using light gauge steel structure along with sustainable & efficient wall panels that are furnished with all Electrical & Sanitary fixtures. White goods can be included as well. Thus, an energy efficient home with better quality & innovation is produced within 90 days.

Prefabricated structures are durable, engineered to endure harsh conditions and long-term use, with quality on par with conventional buildings. A minimum lifespan of 25-30 years is ensured at all remote locations as well.

Modular or Prefab houses usually take 2-4 months to complete, decided based on the final design and site visit.

Modular or Prefabricated homes can generally be constructed up to 3-4 stories, with height limited by structural design and engineering capabilities.

Yes, our constructions are typically turn-key projects, meaning they include everything from design to final touches, providing a complete, ready-to-use home upon completion. Furniture and Furnishings are optional.

We believe every home should reflect personal dreams and needs. Our architects are involved in every project, offering customizable designs to meet individual preferences, thus we are happy to deliver customised designs.

All of our products are designed taking structural stability criteria into account. However, if you need a Structural Stability Certificate for your house, please let the Sales Manager know and it will be taken care of.

Prefabrication reduces waste and energy consumption, leading to a cleaner and healthier indoor environment.

Yes, our Prefabricated structures can be constructed on the rooftop. You can choose among the rooftop layouts and designs and customized rooftop design can also be done.

Sanitary fittings: We provide branded CP fittings by Jaquar/ Parryware/Cera. Ceramic fittings can be chosen among Jaquar/ Glocera/ Parryware. Electric fittings: We provide branded fans, light fittings, MCBs, fire resistant cables and switches. Crompton, Havells – Fan. Legrand/ Goldmedal switches. Polycab cables.

We offer UPVC sliding glass doors and window frames by German Brands – Salamander/ Encraft with lock accessories. For doors, Nishtha Essentials offer Post Foam door frames with solid wooden doors finished with Merino laminate. Doors and Windows are customizable as per client’s choice and budget.

The steel structure is made of MS sections of 100 X 100/150 X 150 mm or ISMC 150 mm & ISMB 200 mm - with support framing of Galvanized steel members for wall panel supports. Internal and external walls are made of 10 mm thickness fibre cement boards insulated with 50 mm thickness glass wool. Roofing is done with Shingles(Imported roofing) from Brands – GAF/ IKO – U.S.A

Yes, the house can be easily dismantled and relocated depending on need, as 60% of the material is recoverable. Only plinth, civil work and finishing items are non-recoverable.

Yes, the customer has to provide the following things: Septic tank for bathroom and kitchen Electric and water supply connections up to prefab house. Space for material storage and team’s basic accommodation. Our site development services can be availed by customer for the same.
