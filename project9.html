HIMALAYAN WOOD RESORT – SHIMLA, HIMACHAL PRADESH

Himalayan Wood Resort – Shimla, Himachal Pradesh is a charming collection of wooden cottages set in the serene hills of Shimla. Completed in 2021, the resort covers a construction area of 1200 sq. ft., offering guests a cozy and rustic retreat amidst the natural beauty of the Himalayas. The wooden architecture perfectly complements the surrounding landscape, creating a harmonious blend of modern comfort and traditional mountain living. Designed with sustainability in mind, the cottages provide an eco-friendly escape, ideal for those seeking tranquility and a connection to nature. With its warm and inviting atmosphere, the Himalayan Wood Resort offers a peaceful getaway in one of India's most scenic destinations.
